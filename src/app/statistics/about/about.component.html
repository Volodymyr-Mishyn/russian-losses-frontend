The Goal of This Site

To bring additional awareness to the ongoing War in Ukraine and to demonstrate the strength and effectiveness of Ukraine's defense against russian occupying forces.

Stand with Ukraine:  Your support can make a difference  in this crucial fight for freedom and sovereignty.

How this site works

This site is group of applications.

Scrapper app for scrapping data of russian losses from two sources: Ministry of defense of Ukraine and Oryx OSINT : Scrapper code

Backend app for processing storing and providing access to data: Backend code

Frontend app for displaying data You are seeing in front of You: Frontend code

Backend Node.js app periodically on schedule runs scrapper data and persists it. Frontend app is Angular Application on bleeding-edge Angular17 with brand new SSR support. Most data grouping is performed on frontend to minimize network traffic.
